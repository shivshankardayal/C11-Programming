Chapter 21. Alternative spellings <iso646.h>

The header <iso646.h> defines the following eleven macros (on the left) that expand to the corresponding tokens (on the right):

    and &&
    and_eq &=
    bitand &
    bitor |
    compl ~
    not !
    not_eq !=
    or ||
    or_eq |=
    xor ^
    xor_eq ^=
